Entropy

A solo abstract strategy game. Move cards through the 4x4 grid using chess piece-like movements to sort cards into the four foundation stacks. Minimize Entropy by building as close to perfectly-ascending stacks as you can.

Objective: Minimize Entropy Build ascending foundation sequences. Empty foundations may begin with any card for 0 entropy. Every later foundation placement adds entropy based on how far it misses the ideal next rank (6->7 adds 0 entropy, 6->8 adds +1 entropy, and so on).
How to play
Cards move along the grid like chess pieces according to suit (below). Select a card, then click a highlighted grid space or foundation slot to move it. Cards are locked once placed in a foundation slot. A card from the draw pile will fill empty slots on the back row.
♠ SpadesPawn: Forward +1; forward/diagonal into Foundation
♣ ClubsBishop: Diagonals
♦ DiamondsKnight: L-Shape
♥ HeartsRook: Rows/Columns
Q; Any SuitQueen Movement
K; Any SuitKing Movement
Sliding pieces cannot pass through occupied cards. Knights jump. C3 starts empty. The back row refills whenever a card moves out of it. Foundation order cycles A → 2 → ... → K → A.
